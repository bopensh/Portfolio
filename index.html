Ben Openshaw | Shopify E-commerce Specialist based in Surrey, UK.

I currently work as an e-commerce manager for a Shopify Plus merchant. I also offer freelance services for store builds, theme customisation, and ongoing support. You can find an overview of the services I offer below.

Services

Store Setup & Migrations

A comprehensive setup service for businesses launching a new store or migrating from another platform. My process focuses on creating a store that is technically sound and fits your business needs. This includes secure data migration, theme selection and configuration, product and collection setup, payment and shipping configuration, and installation of essential apps.

Theme Customisation

Modifying your Shopify theme to align with your brand identity and enhance user experience. This ranges from CSS styling adjustments and layout changes to developing new custom sections based on your specific requirements.

App Installation & Configuration

Assistance in selecting and implementing the right Shopify apps to add specific functionality to your store. This includes apps for customer reviews, subscriptions, enhanced search, and marketing, ensuring they are configured correctly and integrated with your theme.

Ongoing Maintenance & Support

A retainer service for businesses requiring regular assistance with their Shopify store. Services include applying theme updates, making content changes, performing proactive maintenance checks, and providing general support. Training for your team can also be included.

Theme Troubleshooting

Resolving front-end visual and layout issues within your Shopify theme. This service addresses problems such as layout inconsistencies, CSS bugs, and other display errors that impact user experience.
